Privacy Policy for CoSpace

1. Information We Collect

We collect and process the following information:

  • Organization details (name, logo, domain)
  • Employee information (name, email, role, department)
  • Booking data (room reservations, gaming facilities)
  • Complaint and help desk tickets
  • System logs and audit trails

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information is used for:

  • Managing organizational access and permissions
  • Facilitating room and facility bookings
  • Processing help desk requests
  • Sending notifications and updates
  • Generating usage reports and analytics

3. Data Security

We protect your data through:

  • Multi-tenant architecture with data isolation
  • Secure authentication and authorization
  • Role-based access control
  • Regular security audits
  • Encrypted data transmission

4. Data Sharing

We share data only:

  • Within your organization based on role permissions
  • With third-party services essential for operations (email/SMS)
  • When required by law

5. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request data corrections
  • Export your data
  • Request data deletion
